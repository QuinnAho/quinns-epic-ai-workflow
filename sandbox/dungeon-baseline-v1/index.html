Enter The Keep

This v0 build uses a single embedded dungeon blueprint for walls, doors, keys, enemies, and the minimap. Click below to lock the mouse and step inside.

  • Explore: The keep branches before the first gate, so look for the archive key first.
  • Fight smart: Enemies patrol until they see you, then they push through the grid toward your tile.
  • Progress: Each locked gate costs one key and opens permanently.
